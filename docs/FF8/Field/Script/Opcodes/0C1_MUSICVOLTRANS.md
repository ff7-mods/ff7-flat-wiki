---
title: 0C1_MUSICVOLTRANS
---

-   Opcode: **0x0C1**
-   Short name: **MUSICVOLTRANS**
-   Long name: Music Volume Transition

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

  
*? (always 0 or 1)*

*Transition time (in frames)*

*Final volume (0-127)*

**MUSICVOLTRANS**

#### Description

Transition the volume of the area's music over the specified duration.
